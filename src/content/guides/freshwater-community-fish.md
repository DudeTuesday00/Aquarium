## What Makes a Good Community Fish?

Good community fish are peaceful, adaptable, and easy to feed. They also tolerate small mistakes better than more sensitive or territorial species.

## Strong Beginner Picks

- tetras
- rasboras
- corydoras
- guppies

## Stocking Advice

Keep fish in compatible groups and avoid mixing highly nippy species with slow or long-finned fish. Stable stocking matters more than trying to maximize the fish count.
