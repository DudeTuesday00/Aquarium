## Bettas Need More Than a Bowl

Bettas do best in heated, filtered aquariums with low flow and enough room to establish a calm environment.

## Core Setup Tips

- use at least a 5 to 10 gallon tank
- avoid strong filter output
- include resting spots and cover
- keep the water warm and stable

## Feeding and Maintenance

Bettas benefit from controlled feeding, consistent water changes, and simple layouts that are easy to clean.
