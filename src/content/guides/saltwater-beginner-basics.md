## Saltwater Takes More Discipline

Saltwater aquariums can be beautiful, but they demand more precise water preparation, testing, and maintenance than freshwater systems.

## Expect Extra Equipment

- salt mix
- refractometer or salinity testing
- stronger filtration planning
- stricter water-change habits

## Best First Move

Start with a simple fish-only saltwater setup rather than trying to build a complex reef immediately.
