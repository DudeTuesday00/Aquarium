## Which Type of Aquarium Fits You Best?

Freshwater tanks are usually the best entry point for home and small-office users because the setup cost is lower and the learning curve is friendlier.

## Freshwater Advantages

- lower startup cost
- easier water management
- wider range of beginner-safe fish
- simpler equipment choices

## Saltwater Advantages

- more dramatic fish and reef visuals
- stronger specialty appeal
- better fit for hobbyists who want a deeper equipment and chemistry challenge

## Beginner Rule of Thumb

If you want the highest chance of success in your first setup, choose freshwater unless you are specifically motivated by saltwater livestock and are comfortable with more maintenance discipline.
