## Why Filter Choice Matters

The right filter affects noise, maintenance time, water clarity, and fish safety.

## Common Filter Types

### Sponge Filters

Great for simple, quiet, beginner-friendly setups.

### Hang-On-Back Filters

Flexible for many home tanks and easy to maintain.

### Canister Filters

Best for larger or more demanding aquariums where stronger filtration and cleaner presentation matter.

## Matching Filter to Goal

Choose based on tank size, livestock, maintenance tolerance, and whether you care most about simplicity, performance, or appearance.
