## Cichlids Need Planning

Cichlids reward owners with personality and activity, but they need more attention to tank size, compatibility, and territory.

## Before You Start

- choose species carefully
- avoid random mixing
- use stronger filtration
- provide hardscape and structure

## Who This Fits Best

Cichlid tanks are a better fit once you are comfortable managing fish behavior and stocking boundaries.
